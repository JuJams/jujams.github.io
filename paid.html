Home Made Wanna Try

I waste all my money on these

I love basil fried rice. It is so easy to make and tastes so good. I get it with some veggies and tofu.

I get this korean bokki chicken from a local restaurant. It is so spicy and tasty. I love to have it with rice or noodles.

I love drunken noodles. I get it with some veggies and tofu. I love to have it for lunch.

I get brown sugar boba milk tea from HeyTea. It is so good and I love to have it as a dessert.

Mango sticky rice is my favorite dessert. I love to have it after dinner. It is so sweet and tasty.

Tiramisu is usual (yummy) but matchamisu is something I love to have. It is so good and I love to have it after dinner.

Mushroom pasta from Cheescake Factory is so creamy and tasty and my go to when I feel low.

Tofu Kelp Ramen is so filling and tasty. Keeps me full for the whole day.
